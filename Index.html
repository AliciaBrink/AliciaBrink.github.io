Oil on Canvas.

 When looking at an artwork, ask the following questions:  

 

 

 

 

1. What symbols do you see that might represent something important?

2. What do you think this means?

3. What do you think the artist was trying to say?

4. What feelings does this artwork evoke in you?

 

Salvador Dali, 1931. Persistence of Memory.

Oil on Canvas

 

 

When looking at an artwork, ask the following questions:

 

.           

 

 

 

 

 

1. Do you like this artwork? Why/why not?

2. Do you think it is a good artwork? Why/why not?

3. Would you display this in your home? Why/why not?

4. Is this artwork good enough to put in a museum? Why/why not?

5. Why do you think you feel this way (above) about this artwork?
